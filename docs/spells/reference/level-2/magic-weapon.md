# Magic Weapon
- **Level**: 2
- **School**: Transmutation
- **Ritual**: false
- **Classes**: Paladin, Ranger, Sorcerer, Wizard

- **Casting Time Value**: 1
- **Casting Time Unit**: bonus_action
- **Combat Cost**: bonus_action

- **Range Type**: touch
- **Targeting Type**: single
- **Valid Targets**: nonmagical_weapon
- **Line of Sight**: true

- **Verbal**: true
- **Somatic**: true
- **Material**: false

- **Duration Type**: timed
- **Duration Value**: 1
- **Duration Unit**: hour
- **Concentration**: false

- **Effect Type**: BUFF
- **Save Stat**: None
- **Save Outcome**: none

- **Description**: You touch a nonmagical weapon. Until the spell ends, that weapon becomes a magic weapon with a +1 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls. If the weapon isn't magical, it counts as a magic weapon for the duration.
- **Higher Levels**: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, the bonus increases to +2. When you use a spell slot of 6th level or higher, the bonus increases to +3.
- **Source**: PHB 2024 p.295
- **Status**: Complete
