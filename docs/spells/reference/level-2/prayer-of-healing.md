# Prayer of Healing
- **Level**: 2
- **School**: Abjuration
- **Ritual**: false
- **Classes**: Cleric, Paladin

- **Casting Time Value**: 10
- **Casting Time Unit**: minute
- **Combat Cost**: action

- **Range Type**: ranged
- **Range Distance**: 30
- **Targeting Type**: multi
- **Targeting Max**: 6
- **Valid Targets**: creatures
- **Line of Sight**: true

- **Verbal**: true
- **Somatic**: false
- **Material**: false

- **Duration Type**: instantaneous
- **Concentration**: false

- **Effect Type**: HEALING
- **Save Stat**: None
- **Save Outcome**: none
- **Damage Dice**: 2d8
- **Damage Type**: Healing

- **Description**: Up to six creatures of your choice that you can see within range each regain hit points equal to 2d8 + your spellcasting ability modifier. This spell has no effect on Undead or Constructs.
- **Higher Levels**: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the healing increases by 1d8 for each slot level above 2nd.
- **Source**: PHB 2024 p.307
- **Status**: Complete
