# Pass without Trace
- **Level**: 2
- **School**: Abjuration
- **Ritual**: false
- **Classes**: Druid, Ranger

- **Casting Time Value**: 1
- **Casting Time Unit**: action
- **Combat Cost**: action

- **Range Type**: self
- **Targeting Type**: area
- **Area Shape**: emanation
- **Area Size**: 30
- **Valid Targets**: creatures
- **Line of Sight**: false

- **Verbal**: true
- **Somatic**: true
- **Material**: true
- **Material Description**: ashes from a burned leaf of mistletoe and a sprig of spruce
- **Material Cost GP**: 0
- **Consumed**: false

- **Duration Type**: timed
- **Duration Value**: 1
- **Duration Unit**: hour
- **Concentration**: true

- **Effect Type**: BUFF
- **Save Stat**: None
- **Save Outcome**: none

- **Description**: A veil of shadows and silence radiates from you, masking you and your companions from detection. For the duration, each creature you choose within 30 feet of you (including you) has a +10 bonus to Dexterity (Stealth) checks and can't be tracked except by magical means. A creature that receives this bonus leaves behind no tracks or other traces of its passage.
- **Higher Levels**: None
- **Source**: PHB 2024 p.303
- **Status**: Complete
