# Charm Monster
- **Level**: 4
- **School**: Enchantment
- **Ritual**: false
- **Classes**: Bard, Druid, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard

- **Casting Time Value**: 1
- **Casting Time Unit**: action
- **Combat Cost**: action

- **Range Type**: ranged
- **Range Distance**: 30
- **Targeting Type**: single
- **Valid Targets**: creatures
- **Line of Sight**: true

- **Verbal**: true
- **Somatic**: true
- **Material**: false

- **Duration Type**: timed
- **Duration Value**: 1
- **Duration Unit**: hour
- **Concentration**: false

- **Effect Type**: STATUS
- **Save Stat**: Wisdom
- **Save Outcome**: negates
- **Conditions Applied**: Charmed

- **Description**: One creature you can see within range makes a Wisdom saving throw. It does so with Advantage if you or your allies are fighting it. On a failed save, the target has the Charmed condition until the spell ends or until you or your allies damage it. The Charmed creature is Friendly to you. When the spell ends, the target knows it was Charmed by you.
- **Higher Levels**: You can target one additional creature for each spell slot level above 4.
- **Source**: PHB 2024
- **Status**: Complete
