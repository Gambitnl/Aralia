- **School**: Conjuration

- **Classes**: Ranger

- **Effect Type**: DAMAGE
- **Save Stat**: Dexterity
- **Save Outcome**: half
- **Damage Dice**: 1d10
- **Damage Type**: Piercing

- **Description**: As you hit the creature, this spell creates a rain of thorns that sprouts from your Ranged weapon or ammunition.
