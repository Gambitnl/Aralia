# Chromatic Orb
- **Level**: 1
- **School**: Evocation
- **Ritual**: false
- **Classes**: Sorcerer, Wizard

- **Casting Time Value**: 1
- **Casting Time Unit**: action
- **Combat Cost**: action

- **Range Type**: ranged
- **Range Distance**: 90
- **Targeting Type**: single
- **Valid Targets**: creatures
- **Line of Sight**: true

- **Verbal**: true
- **Somatic**: true
- **Material**: true
- **Material Description**: a diamond worth 50+ GP
- **Material Cost GP**: 50
- **Consumed**: false

- **Duration Type**: instantaneous
- **Concentration**: false

- **Effect Type**: DAMAGE
- **Attack Roll**: ranged
- **Damage Dice**: 3d8
- **Damage Type**: Acid/Cold/Fire/Lightning/Poison/Thunder

- **Description**: You hurl a 4-inch-diameter sphere of energy at a creature that you can see within range. You choose Acid, Cold, Fire, Lightning, Poison, or Thunder for the type of orb you create, and then make a ranged spell attack against the target. On a hit, the creature takes 3d8 damage of the type you chose. If you roll the same number on two or more of the d8s, the orb leaps to a different target of your choice within 30 feet of the original target. Make a new attack roll against the new target, and make a new damage roll. The orb can leap only once per casting, unless you cast this spell with a level 2+ slot.
- **Higher Levels**: Damage increases by 1d8 per slot level above 1. The orb can leap a maximum number of times equal to the slot level used (e.g., up to 2 leaps with a level 2 slot). A creature can be targeted only once per casting.
- **Source**: PHB 2024 p.249
- **Status**: Complete
