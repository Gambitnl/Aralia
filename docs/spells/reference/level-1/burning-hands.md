# Burning Hands
- **Level**: 1
- **School**: Evocation
- **Ritual**: false
- **Classes**: Sorcerer, Wizard

- **Casting Time Value**: 1
- **Casting Time Unit**: action
- **Combat Cost**: action

- **Range Type**: self
- **Targeting Type**: area
- **Area Shape**: cone
- **Area Size**: 15
- **Valid Targets**: creatures
- **Line of Sight**: false

- **Verbal**: true
- **Somatic**: true
- **Material**: false

- **Duration Type**: instantaneous
- **Concentration**: false

- **Effect Type**: DAMAGE
- **Save Stat**: Dexterity
- **Save Outcome**: half
- **Damage Dice**: 3d6
- **Damage Type**: Fire

- **Description**: As you hold your hands with thumbs touching and fingers spread, a thin sheet of flames shoots forth from your outstretched fingertips. Each creature in a 15-foot Cone must make a Dexterity saving throw. A creature takes 3d6 Fire damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. The fire ignites any flammable objects in the area that aren't being worn or carried.
- **Higher Levels**: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d6 for each slot level above 1st.
- **Source**: PHB 2024 p.248
- **Status**: Complete
