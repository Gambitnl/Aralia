# Thunderous Smite
- **Level**: 1
- **School**: Evocation
- **Ritual**: false
- **Classes**: Paladin

- **Casting Time Value**: 1
- **Casting Time Unit**: bonus_action
- **Combat Cost**: bonus_action
- **Reaction Trigger**: before the spell ends

- **Range Type**: self
- **Targeting Type**: single
- **Valid Targets**: creature_hit
- **Line of Sight**: true

- **Verbal**: true
- **Somatic**: false
- **Material**: false

- **Duration Type**: timed
- **Duration Value**: 1
- **Duration Unit**: minute
- **Concentration**: true

- **Effect Type**: DAMAGE
- **Save Stat**: Strength
- **Save Outcome**: negates
- **Damage Dice**: 2d6
- **Damage Type**: Thunder
- **Conditions Applied**: Prone

- **Description**: The first time you hit with a melee weapon attack during this spell's duration, your weapon rings with thunder that is audible within 300 feet of you, and the attack deals an extra 2d6 Thunder damage to the target. Additionally, if the target is a creature, it must succeed on a Strength saving throw or be pushed 10 feet away from you and knocked Prone.
- **Higher Levels**: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the extra damage increases by 1d6 for each slot level above 1st.
- **Source**: PHB 2024 p.334
- **Status**: Complete
