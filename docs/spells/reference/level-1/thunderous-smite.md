# Thunderous Smite
- **Level**: 1
- **School**: Evocation
- **Ritual**: false
- **Classes**: Paladin

- **Casting Time Value**: 1
- **Casting Time Unit**: bonus_action
- **Combat Cost**: bonus_action

- **Range Type**: self
- **Targeting Type**: single
- **Valid Targets**: creatures
- **Line of Sight**: true

- **Verbal**: true
- **Somatic**: false
- **Material**: false

- **Duration Type**: instantaneous
- **Concentration**: false

- **Effect Type**: DAMAGE, STATUS_CONDITION, MOVEMENT
- **Save Stat**: Strength
- **Save Outcome**: negates_condition
- **Damage Dice**: 2d6
- **Damage Type**: Thunder
- **Conditions Applied**: Prone

- **Description**: Your strike rings with thunder that is audible within 300 feet of you, and the target takes an extra 2d6 Thunder damage from the attack. Additionally, if the target is a creature, it must succeed on a Strength saving throw or be pushed 10 feet away from you and have the Prone condition.
- **Higher Levels**: Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot. The damage increases by 1d6 for each spell slot level above 1.
