# Create or Destroy Water
- **Level**: 1
- **School**: Transmutation
- **Ritual**: false
- **Classes**: Cleric, Druid

- **Casting Time Value**: 1
- **Casting Time Unit**: action
- **Combat Cost**: action

- **Range Type**: ranged
- **Range Distance**: 30
- **Targeting Type**: area
- **Area Shape**: cube
- **Area Size**: 30
- **Valid Targets**: point
- **Line of Sight**: false

- **Verbal**: true
- **Somatic**: true
- **Material**: true
- **Material Description**: a drop of water (create) or a few grains of sand (destroy)
- **Material Cost GP**: 0
- **Consumed**: false

- **Duration Type**: instantaneous
- **Concentration**: false

- **Effect Type**: UTILITY
- **Save Stat**: None
- **Save Outcome**: none

- **Description**: You either create or destroy water. Create Water: You create up to 10 gallons of clean water within range in an open container. Alternatively, the water falls as rain in a 30-foot Cube within range. Destroy Water: You destroy up to 10 gallons of water in an open container within range. Alternatively, you destroy fog in a 30-foot Cube within range.
- **Higher Levels**: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, you create or destroy 10 additional gallons of water, or the size of the Cube increases by 5 feet, for each slot level above 1st.
- **Source**: PHB 2024 p.258
- **Status**: Complete
