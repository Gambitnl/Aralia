- **Level**: 1
- **School**: Conjuration
- **Ritual**: false
- **Classes**: Druid, Ranger
- **Sub-Classes**: Cleric - Festus Domain, Cleric - Community Domain (HCS)

- **Casting Time Value**: 1

- **Targeting Type**: self
- **Valid Targets**: self
- **Line of Sight**: false

- **Verbal**: true

- **Material Description**: a sprig of mistletoe
- **Material Cost GP**: 0
- **Consumed**: false

- **Duration Type**: timed
- **Duration Value**: 24
- **Duration Unit**: hour
- **Concentration**: false

- **Effect Type**: UTILITY
- **Utility Type**: creation
- **Save Stat**: None
- **Save Outcome**: none

- **Description**: Ten berries appear in your hand and are infused with magic for the duration. A creature can take a Bonus Action to eat one berry.
