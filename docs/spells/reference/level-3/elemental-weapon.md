# Elemental Weapon
- **Level**: 3
- **School**: Transmutation
- **Ritual**: false
- **Classes**: Artificer, Druid, Paladin, Ranger

- **Casting Time Value**: 1
- **Casting Time Unit**: action
- **Combat Cost**: action

- **Range Type**: touch
- **Targeting Type**: single
- **Valid Targets**: nonmagical_weapon
- **Line of Sight**: true

- **Verbal**: true
- **Somatic**: true
- **Material**: false

- **Duration Type**: timed
- **Duration Value**: 1
- **Duration Unit**: hour
- **Concentration**: true

- **Effect Type**: BUFF
- **Save Stat**: None
- **Save Outcome**: none
- **Damage Dice**: 1d4
- **Damage Type**: Acid/Cold/Fire/Lightning/Thunder

- **Description**: A nonmagical weapon you touch becomes a magic weapon. Choose one of the following damage types: Acid, Cold, Fire, Lightning, or Thunder. For the duration, the weapon has a +1 bonus to attack rolls and deals an extra 1d4 damage of the chosen type when it hits.
- **Higher Levels**: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 5th or 6th level, the bonus to attack rolls increases to +2 and the extra damage increases to 2d4. When you use a spell slot of 7th level or higher, the bonus increases to +3 and the extra damage increases to 3d4.
- **Source**: PHB 2024 p.267
- **Status**: Complete
