# Create Food and Water
- **Level**: 3
- **School**: Conjuration
- **Ritual**: false
- **Classes**: Cleric, Paladin

- **Casting Time Value**: 1
- **Casting Time Unit**: action
- **Combat Cost**: action

- **Range Type**: ranged
- **Range Distance**: 30
- **Targeting Type**: point
- **Valid Targets**: location
- **Line of Sight**: true

- **Verbal**: true
- **Somatic**: true
- **Material**: false

- **Duration Type**: instantaneous
- **Concentration**: false

- **Effect Type**: UTILITY
- **Save Stat**: None
- **Save Outcome**: none

- **Description**: You create 45 pounds of food and 30 gallons of fresh water on the ground or in containers within range, enough to sustain up to fifteen humanoids or five steeds for 24 hours. The food is bland but nourishing, and spoils if uneaten after 24 hours. The water is clean and doesn't go bad.
- **Higher Levels**: None
- **Source**: PHB 2024 p.258
- **Status**: Complete
