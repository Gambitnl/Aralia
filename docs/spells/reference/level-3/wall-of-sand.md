# Wall of Sand
- **Level**: 3
- **School**: Evocation
- **Ritual**: false
- **Classes**: Wizard

- **Casting Time Value**: 1
- **Casting Time Unit**: action
- **Combat Cost**: action

- **Range Type**: ranged
- **Range Distance**: 90
- **Targeting Type**: area
- **Area Shape**: wall
- **Area Size**: 30
- **Valid Targets**: point
- **Line of Sight**: true

- **Verbal**: true
- **Somatic**: true
- **Material**: true
- **Material Description**: a handful of sand
- **Material Cost GP**: 0
- **Consumed**: false

- **Duration Type**: timed
- **Duration Value**: 10
- **Duration Unit**: minute
- **Concentration**: true

- **Effect Type**: UTILITY
- **Save Stat**: None
- **Save Outcome**: none
- **Conditions Applied**: Blinded

- **Description**: You create a wall of swirling sand on the ground at a point you can see within range. You can make the wall up to 30 feet long, 10 feet high, and 10 feet thick, and it vanishes when the spell ends. It blocks line of sight but not movement. A creature is Blinded while in the wall's space and must spend 3 feet of movement for every 1 foot it moves there.
