# Flame Arrows
- **Level**: 3
- **School**: Transmutation
- **Ritual**: false
- **Classes**: Artificer, Druid, Ranger, Sorcerer, Wizard

- **Casting Time Value**: 1
- **Casting Time Unit**: action
- **Combat Cost**: action

- **Range Type**: touch
- **Targeting Type**: single
- **Valid Targets**: quiver_of_arrows
- **Line of Sight**: true

- **Verbal**: true
- **Somatic**: true
- **Material**: false

- **Duration Type**: timed
- **Duration Value**: 1
- **Duration Unit**: hour
- **Concentration**: true

- **Effect Type**: BUFF
- **Save Stat**: None
- **Save Outcome**: none
- **Damage Dice**: 1d6
- **Damage Type**: Fire

- **Description**: You touch a quiver containing arrows or bolts. When a target is hit by a ranged weapon attack using a piece of ammunition drawn from the quiver, the target takes an extra 1d6 Fire damage. The spell's magic ends on the piece of ammunition when it hits or misses, and the spell ends when twelve pieces of ammunition have been drawn from the quiver.
- **Higher Levels**: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, the number of pieces of ammunition you can affect increases by two for each slot level above 3rd.
- **Source**: PHB 2024 p.275
- **Status**: Complete
