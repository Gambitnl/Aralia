- **Level**: 6
- **School**: Conjuration
- **Ritual**: false
- **Classes**: Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard

- **Casting Time Value**: 1
- **Casting Time Unit**: action
- **Combat Cost**: action

- **Range Type**: ranged
- **Range Distance**: 30
- **Targeting Type**: area
- **Valid Targets**: creatures
- **Line of Sight**: true

- **Verbal**: true
- **Somatic**: false
- **Material**: false

- **Duration Type**: instantaneous
- **Concentration**: false

- **Effect Type**: UTILITY
- **Utility Type**: teleportation
- **Save Stat**: Wisdom
- **Save Outcome**: negates

- **Description**: The air quivers around up to five creatures of your choice that you can see within range. An unwilling creature must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw to resist this spell.
