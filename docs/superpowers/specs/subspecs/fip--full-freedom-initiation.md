# Sub-spec: Full-freedom combat initiation

**Parent:** `../2026-07-02-fight-in-place-combat-design.md` · **Status:** specced, not built.

## Decision
The player can initiate combat against ANY NPC from slice 1 (BG3-style total agency), not just designated hostiles. Consequences (guards, kin joining, reputation, economic fallout) arrive with the world-reactions system; the interim consequence-light window is accepted (same posture as beauty-before-perf). Pairs with the non-lethal toggle and death rules (see world-reactions sub-specs).

## Open
- The initiation UX (attack action on any NPC in range? a hostile-intent mode?).
- Party consent / companions' reaction to unprovoked aggression (ties to NPC consent, currently deferred in travel work).
