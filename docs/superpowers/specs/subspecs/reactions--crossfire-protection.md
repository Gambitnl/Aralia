# Sub-spec: Crossfire protection + AI arbiter override

**Parent:** `../2026-07-02-fight-in-place-combat-design.md` · **Status:** specced, not built.

## Decision
Civilians caught in the crossfire CANNOT be accidentally killed by NPC hostiles (stray AoE, missed shots — they drop to a downed/fled state instead). EXCEPTION: the AI arbiter may deliberately rule a civilian death as an authored story hook. Tragedy is authored, never random.
