# Sub-spec: Non-lethal damage toggle

**Parent:** `../2026-07-02-fight-in-place-combat-design.md` · **Status:** specced, not built.

## Decision
Player damage carries a non-lethal toggle (the 5e melee knock-out rule, generalized per Remy's call): reducing a civilian to 0 HP knocks out unless the player explicitly chose lethal. Killing a named NPC is always a deliberate act — accidental tragedy by the player is impossible, preserving full-freedom initiation without whoopsie murders.

## Open
- Toggle granularity (global stance vs per-attack).
- 5e fidelity question: RAW restricts knock-out to melee — does the toggle extend to ranged/spells (house rule) or stay RAW?
- Knocked-out NPC state + recovery (wake time, memory of the assault → relationship hit).
