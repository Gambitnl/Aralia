# Sub-spec: Permanent cascading NPC death

**Parent:** `../2026-07-02-fight-in-place-combat-design.md` · **Status:** specced, not built.

## Decision
A named NPC's death is PERMANENT and cascades through the living-world sim: household grieves (life-event), business closes or passes to kin/apprentice (economy system), relationships update, the town remembers (news/rumor surfaces). Death becomes an event the existing sim processes — households, businesses, life events, and news surfaces all already exist; this wires death into them.

## Open
- Inheritance rules (kin first? apprentice? business dissolves?).
- Grief/memory representation (rumor entries? dialogue references?).
- Player-visible consequences pacing (immediate vs daily-loop).
