    Grasping plants sprout in a 20-foot square within range, turning the area into difficult terrain. Each creature in the area when you cast must succeed on a Strength save or be restrained until the spell ends. A restrained creature can use an action to make a Strength (Athletics) check against your spell save DC, ending the effect on a success.
