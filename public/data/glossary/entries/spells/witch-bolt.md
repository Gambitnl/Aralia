  <p class="spell-card-description">Hurl a bolt of lightning at a creature within 30 feet. On a hit it takes 1d12 lightning damage, and while you concentrate you can use your action on later turns to automatically deal another 1d12 to the same target. The arc ends if the target leaves range, gains total cover, or you take another action. Higher slots add 1d12 to the initial and sustained damage.</p>
