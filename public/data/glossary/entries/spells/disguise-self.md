  <p class="spell-card-description">
    You make yourself, including your clothing, armor, weapons, and other belongings on your person, look different until the spell ends. You can seem 1 foot shorter or taller and can appear heavier or lighter but must keep the same basic limb arrangement. Physical contact passes through the illusion.
  </p>
  <p class="spell-card-description">
    To discern the disguise, a creature must use the Study action to inspect your appearance and succeed on an Intelligence (Investigation) check against your spell save DC.
