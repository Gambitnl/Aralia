## Mage Hand

- **Level:** Cantrip (0)
- **School:** Conjuration
- **Casting Time:** 1 action
- **Range:** 30 feet
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** 1 minute

### Effect
A spectral, floating hand appears at a point you choose within range. The hand lasts for the duration or until you dismiss it as an action. It can manipulate an object, open an unlocked door or container, stow or retrieve an item from an open container, or pour the contents out of a vial.
