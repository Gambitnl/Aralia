## Barkskin

- **Level:** 2
- **School:** Transmutation
- **Casting Time:** 1 action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S, M (a handful of oak bark)
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 hour

### Effect
You touch a willing creature. For the duration, the target’s skin has a rough, bark-like appearance, and its AC can’t be less than 16, regardless of what armor it is wearing.
