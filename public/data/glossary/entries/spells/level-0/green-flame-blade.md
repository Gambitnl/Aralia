  <p class="spell-card-description">
    As part of casting the spell, make a melee attack with a weapon against one creature within 5 feet. On a hit, the target takes the attack's normal damage, and green fire leaps to a second creature of your choice within 5 feet of the target, dealing fire damage equal to your spellcasting ability modifier.
  </p>
  <p class="spell-card-description">
    <strong>Cantrip Upgrade.</strong> At 5th level, the melee attack deals an extra 1d8 fire damage to the primary target, and the secondary damage also gains 1d8. Both increase by 1d8 at 11th and 17th level.
