## Friends

- **Level:** Cantrip (0)
- **School:** Enchantment
- **Casting Time:** 1 action
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** S, M (a small amount of makeup applied to the face as this spell is cast)
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute

### Effect
For the duration, you have advantage on Charisma checks directed at one creature of your choice that isn't hostile toward you.

### Aftermath
When the spell ends, the creature realizes you used magic to influence its mood and may become hostile depending on the situation.
