## Sword Burst

- **Level:** Cantrip (0)
- **School:** Conjuration
- **Casting Time:** 1 action
- **Range:** Self (5-foot radius)
- **Components:** V
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

### Effect
You create a momentary circle of spectral blades that sweep around you. Each creature within 5 feet of you must succeed on a Dexterity saving throw or take 1d6 force damage.
