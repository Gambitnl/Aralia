# Primal Savagery

<div class="spell-card">
  <div class="spell-header">
    <div class="spell-level">Transmutation Cantrip</div>
    <div class="spell-meta">
      <span class="casting-time">1 Action</span>
      <span class="range">Self (5-foot radius)</span>
      <span class="components">S</span>
      <span class="duration">Instantaneous</span>
    </div>
  </div>
  <div class="spell-description">
    <p>You channel primal magic to cause your teeth or fingernails to sharpen, ready to deliver a corrosive attack. Make a melee spell attack against one creature within 5 feet of you. On a hit, the target takes 1d10 acid damage. After you make the attack, your body returns to normal.</p>
    <p><strong>At Higher Levels.</strong> This spell's damage increases by 1d10 when you reach 5th level (2d10), 11th level (3d10), and 17th level (4d10).</p>
  </div>

- **Effect**: 1d10 Acid damage (scales)
