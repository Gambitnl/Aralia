  <p class="spell-card-description">
    For the duration, you sense the presence of magical effects within 30 feet of yourself. If you sense magic, you can take the Magic action to see a faint aura around any visible creature or object in the area that bears magic, and if an effect was created by a spell, you learn its school. The spell is blocked by 1 foot of stone, dirt, or wood; 1 inch of metal; or a thin sheet of lead.
