  <p class="spell-card-description">
    You attempt to compel a creature into a duel. One creature you can see within range makes a Wisdom saving throw. On a failed save, it has Disadvantage on attack rolls against creatures other than you and cannot willingly move more than 30 feet away from you. The spell ends if you attack a different creature, cast a spell on another enemy, an ally damages the target, or you end your turn more than 30 feet away from it.
