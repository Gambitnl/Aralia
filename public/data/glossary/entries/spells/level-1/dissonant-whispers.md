    One creature you can see within range hears a discordant melody in its mind. It makes a Wisdom saving throw, taking 3d6 psychic damage on a failed save or half as much on a success.
  </p>
  <p class="spell-card-description">
    On a failed save, the target must immediately use its reaction, if available, to move as far away from you as it can by the safest route.
  </p>
