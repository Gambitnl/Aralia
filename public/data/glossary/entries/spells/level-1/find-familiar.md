  <p class="spell-card-description">
    You summon a familiar spirit in an eligible beast form. It acts independently, cannot attack, uses its own initiative, and disappears at 0 HP. You can communicate telepathically within 100 feet, see through its senses as a bonus action, and have it deliver touch spells within 100 feet by using its reaction. You can dismiss or resummon it to a pocket dimension with a magic action, and you can only have one familiar at a time.
