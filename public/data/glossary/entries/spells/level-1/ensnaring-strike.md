  <p class="spell-card-description">
    After you hit a creature with a weapon, it makes a Strength save (Large+ has advantage). On a failed save, the target is restrained by vines. While restrained, it takes 1d6 piercing damage at the start of each of its turns. A creature can use an action to make a Strength (Athletics) check against your spell save DC, ending the effect on a success.
  </p>
  <p class="spell-card-description">
    <strong>At Higher Levels.</strong> The start-of-turn damage increases by 1d6 for each slot level above 1st.
